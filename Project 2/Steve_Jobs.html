Steve Jobs Fan Website

Steve Jobs was co-founder and CEO of Apple Inc. He is considered to be a brilliant businessman, as well as a great tech visionary of our time.

Steve Jobs has a fascinating story. After dropping out of college, he partnered with his friend, Steve Wozniac to start Apple in 1967 at 21 years old. The two gained a lot of fame and wealth a year later for the Apple II. The Apple II was one of the first mass-produced personal computers. Earlier in 1984, Macintosh was the first mass-produced computer with a GUI '(graphical user interface).' After being ousted of Apple in 1985, he went on to oversee the creation of two new companies: NeXT and Pixar With the success of the movie Toy Story, Steve became a billionaire.

In 1997 Steve returned to Apple. Bill Gates and Microsoft dominated the industry, and Apple was on the brink of extinction. Nonetheless, Steve was a fantastic salesman, and used his business acumen to slowly bring the company back to profitability.
